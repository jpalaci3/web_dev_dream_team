Entertaining audiences with great singing and good, clean humor since 1986!

The Marietta, GA Chapter of the Barbershop Harmony Society, a.k.a. the Big Chicken Chorus, strives to preserve and encourage the singing of Barbershop Harmony in a professional and congenial atmosphere. Our purpose is to enrich the quality of life in our community, engage our audiences, provide musical education and performance opportunities to the youth of our community, and provide musical challenge and reward to our singers.

We welcome any opportunity to apply our hobby of singing four-part harmony, and have traveled extensively to present our brand of entertainment to a variety of audiences, both young and old!

We rehearse Monday evenings from 7:00pm to 9:30pm at Marietta High School. Any exception to this schedule is noted on the calendar.

We invite all men who love to sing to visit us and join us on the risers.

Upcoming Events

  • Celebrating the Gentlemen, June 22, 2019, 3:00 pm

    Gentlemen's Agreement

    On June 22, 2019, we will present Celebrating the Gentlemen, a tribute show honoring Gentlemen’s Agreement, the 1971 International Barbershop Harmony Society Gold Medal winners, and specifically its lead, our own Drayton Justus -- a man who has, for nearly 60 years, served the Barbershop Harmony Society as a performer, chorus director, valued mentor and international president.

    The show will feature many of Gentlemen’s Agreement’s classic renditions to be performed by the Big Chicken Chorus, the Atlanta Vocal Project, the Stone Mountain Chorus and Chicken Little. Featured quartets performing will be Interstate Rivals (the 1987 BHS Gold Medal winners), Category 4 and Quadraphonics.

    In addition, all BHS members are invited to join in Celebrating the Gentlemen by singing with the special Gentlemen’s Gentlemen chorus which will perform on the stage that day.

    The event and a gala afterglow will be held at the newly opened Byers Theatre [view map], a 1000+ seat venue in Sandy Springs, Georgia.
