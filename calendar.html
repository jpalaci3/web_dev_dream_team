June 22, 2019

Celebrating the Gentlemen at 3:00 pm

On June 22, 2019, we will present Celebrating the Gentlemen, a tribute show honoring Gentlemens Agreement, the 1971 International Barbershop Harmony Society Gold Medal winners, and specifically its lead, our own Drayton Justus -- a man who has, for nearly 60 years, served the Barbershop Harmony Society as a performer, chorus director, valued mentor and international president.
